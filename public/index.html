Options Entry & Alert Analysis

Alert Level (Get Ready Zone)

Set alert 0.2%–0.5% below breakout resistance. This is not an entry signal.

Entry Rules (All 3 Required)

  • 5M candle closes above resistance and VWAP
  • Volume > 2x average of previous 10 candles
  • MACD (8,17,9) crosses above signal and histogram turns green

Do Not Enter When

  • Price is only above 9/21 EMA
  • POC remains above current price
  • Green candle has weak volume

15-Minute Breakout Workflow

  • Alert 1 = Breakout level -1%
  • Alert 2 = Volume spike > 2x average
  • Entry = 5M close above breakout + MACD confirmation

Options Tip

Enter after confirmation rather than chasing the first wick. This helps avoid IV spikes and false breakouts.
