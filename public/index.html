Options Entry & Alert Analysis

Alert Level: Set 0.2–0.5% below breakout resistance. This is a get-ready zone, not an entry.

Entry Rules (all required):

  • 5M candle closes above resistance and VWAP
  • Volume > 2x average of last 10 candles
  • MACD (8,17,9) crosses above signal and histogram turns green

Avoid Entries: Above EMA only, POC still overhead, or low-volume green candles.

15M Workflow: Alert 1 = breakout line minus 1%; Alert 2 = 2x volume spike; Entry = 5M close above level + MACD confirmation.
